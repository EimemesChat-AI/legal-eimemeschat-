Privacy Policy

Last Update: June 18, 2026

EimemesChat AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Services. We process your personal data in accordance with applicable Indian data protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Chat Content: This includes the messages, conversations, and any files you upload while interacting with our AI assistant (collectively referred to as "Inputs"). We retain your conversation history to provide a seamless experience, but please be aware that any files you upload are not stored on our servers.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP Address: We collect your IP address for security, analytics, and service optimization.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the Service, including features used and time spent.
  • Cookies and Local Storage: We use cookies and local storage to maintain session state, remember preferences, and improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services
  • Generate AI responses via third‑party providers such as Groq and Google (Gemini) – we may change or add providers over time
  • Store your conversation history
  • Improve and personalize user experience
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • AI Model Providers: Your Inputs are sent to Groq and Google (Gemini) via their APIs to generate responses. They process data according to their own privacy policies (Groq Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy). We may replace or add additional AI providers in the future; any change will be reflected here.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Our hosting provider (Vercel) and database (Firebase) may have access to data as part of providing their services.
  • Authentication: Google authentication is handled by Google; refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. You may delete your account at any time, which will remove your stored data. Chat history is retained to provide continuity of service.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at eimemeschatai@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it.

By using the Services you consent to the transfer of your data to the U.S. and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. We take necessary measures to ensure such transfers comply with applicable Indian data protection laws.
