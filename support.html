Frequently Asked Questions

What is EimemesChat AI? ▼
EimemesChat AI is an independent platform providing AI-powered conversational services with a focus on cultural preservation.
Is EimemesChat free to use? ▼
Yes, the service is completely free. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.
What data do you collect? ▼
When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture. We also collect IP addresses for security purposes and store conversation history. We do not sell personal data. For complete information, please review our Privacy Policy.
Does EimemesChat support the Kuki language? ▼
Currently, the platform operates primarily in English. We are actively working toward implementing Kuki language capabilities.
What are the daily message limits? ▼
Users are limited to 30 messages per day. This limit resets at midnight.
How do I delete my conversations? ▼
You can delete individual conversations or clear all chat history through the Settings panel.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Go to 「Settings」 --> 「Account settings」 --> 「Delete Account」. After deletion, all chat history in the current account will be permanently deleted. Please proceed with caution.
Who develops and maintains this platform? ▼
EimemesChat is an independent project developed by individuals committed to cultural preservation through technology.
