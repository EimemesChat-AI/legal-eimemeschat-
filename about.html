About Us

"Preserving language is preserving identity" — this principle guides our work.

Background

Indigenous and minority language communities have historically been underserved by technological advancement. While global platforms have expanded rapidly, languages such as Kuki have seen limited representation in digital spaces. EimemesChat was developed to address this gap — ensuring that as artificial intelligence becomes ubiquitous, our communities have a presence and a voice.

Mission

To develop accessible AI-powered tools that serve indigenous language communities, with the objective of creating technology that represents and preserves cultural identity. We maintain that language constitutes a fundamental component of identity, and that every community deserves to participate in the digital age on its own terms.

Language Development

Technology for Kuki language processing exists — this is precisely why we are pursuing its integration. The technical challenge lies not in the impossibility of the task, but in the focused effort required to adapt existing models and frameworks to our specific linguistic context. We are actively working toward implementing Kuki language capabilities and welcome collaboration from linguists, speakers, and technologists who share this objective.

Vision

To establish a platform where Kuki speakers — whether fluent or learning — can interact with technology in their own language. We aim to develop a system that not processes the words but respects the cultural context, traditions, and values of the community it serves.

Development & Maintenance

EimemesChat is an independently developed project, built and maintained without corporate backing. This independence allows us to prioritize cultural preservation, user privacy, and accessibility over commercial objectives.

Operational Costs

Maintaining a free service requires ongoing resources:

  • API infrastructure — Each interaction incurs computational costs that scale with usage.
  • Domain and hosting — Ensuring reliable 24/7 availability.
  • Development — Continuous improvement, bug fixes, and feature development.
  • Research — Advancing Kuki language integration.

Development History

The application evolved from a simple prototype through multiple architecture iterations, provider integrations, and stability enhancements. Each update — whether visual refinement, performance optimization, or new feature — represents ongoing commitment to quality and user experience.

Roadmap

  • Current phase (v3.x): Refining user experience, improving reliability, and building community.
  • Near-term (v3.1.0): Implementing Kuki language capabilities.
  • Major update Expanding to support additional indigenous languages.

Transparency

We maintain openness about our capabilities and limitations. The path to comprehensive linguistic representation requires sustained effort and resources. We offer no immediate solutions, only sustained commitment to the communities we serve.
